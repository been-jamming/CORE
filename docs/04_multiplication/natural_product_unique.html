Description: Proving that the product between two natural numbers is unique. After this, we won't need NATURALS_MULTIPLICATION_FUNCTION anymore.

Source:

prove natural_product_unique: *X*Y*Z0*Z1(is_natural_product(X, Y, Z0) & is_natural_product(X, Y, Z1) -> equal_sets(Z0, Z1)){ is_function, zero_identity, product_recursion = expand(naturals_product_function_definition); given |X, Y, Z0, Z1|; implies product_Z0, product_Z1; X_in_NATURALS, Y_in_NATURALS, tuple_condition0 = expand(product_Z0); X_in_NATURALS, Y_in_NATURALS, tuple_condition1 = expand(product_Z1); tuple_T = tuple(X, Y)|T|; T_related_Z0 = tuple_condition0(T)(tuple_T); T_related_Z1 = tuple_condition1(T)(tuple_T); return expand(is_function)(T, Z0, Z1)(T_related_Z0, T_related_Z1); }
